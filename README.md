# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance using PostgreSQL and Power BI. The objective was to identify trends in revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance through data analysis and interactive dashboarding.

The analysis was performed using the Sample Superstore dataset, which contains 9,994 retail transactions across multiple product categories, customer segments, and geographic regions.

---

## Business Objectives

This project was designed to answer the following business questions:

- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- How has sales performance changed over time?
- Which regions contribute the most profit?
- Who are the company's most valuable customers?
- What key performance indicators best measure business performance?

## Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

Technology generated the highest sales revenue among all product categories.

### Sales Growth

Revenue increased consistently between 2014 and 2017, indicating strong business growth over the analyzed period.

### Regional Performance

The West region generated the highest overall profit, while the Central region generated the lowest profit.

### Customer Analysis

A small group of customers contributed a significant portion of total revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention and relationship management.
